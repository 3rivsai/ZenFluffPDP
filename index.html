Our FluffCo Zen Pillow offers the luxury of 5-star hotel sleep at a sensible price. With our custom Fluff™ blends and supportive pillow design, we replicate the plush, supportive feel of hotel pillows you adore, but without the hefty markup.

Question: How are FluffCo Zen Pillows more affordable than hotel brand pillows?

We partner directly with the manufacturers that supply to luxury hotels. This eliminates unnecessary markups and enables us to provide a premium soft pillow at a fraction of the cost of brands like Four Seasons, Ritz Carlton, and Marriott.

Question: Can these pillows help improve my sleep quality?

Yes! Our soft yet firm pillow provides superior comfort and support, helping to alleviate neck pain and enhance overall sleep quality. It’s perfect for side, back, and stomach sleepers alike.

Question: How do I clean and maintain my pillow?

While we recommend avoiding machine washing for our FluffCo Zen Pillow, it comes with a FREE washable 100% cotton protector. For the microfiber pillow itself, spot cleaning or professional dry cleaning is best. Regular airing can also help keep your pillow fresh.

Question: Are these pillows ethically and sustainably made?

Absolutely. We adhere to the Responsible Down Standard (RDS) for all products, ensuring they're humanely and ethically made.
