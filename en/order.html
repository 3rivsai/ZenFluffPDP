    Question:Can you explain the unique benefits of choosing Firm over Soft FluffCo pillows?

    The choice between Firm and Soft FluffCo pillows hinges on your sleeping preferences. Our Firm pillows, packed with more Fluff™, provide enhanced loft and support, ideal for side sleepers needing extra elevation for neck and spinal alignment. Soft pillows, on the other hand, offer a gentler "squish," perfect for back and stomach sleepers craving a cuddlier feel.

    Question:How do FluffCo pillows contribute to a healthier sleep cycle?
